Five-year Cost Comparison

Stakeholder model in GEAV includes all the benefits of aircraft ownership at a fraction of the cost.

There are three cost buckets associated with owning an aircraft.

Acquisition
  • Aircraft
  • Engine reserve
  • Prop reserve
  • Avionics upgrades
Annual
  • Annual
  • Hangar
  • Insurance
  • Sched maint
  • Weather service
Variable/hr
  • Fuel
  • Oil change reserve
  • Engine reserve
  • Prop reserve
  • Regular maintenance

GEAV Financial Advantage

While variable costs are based on your flying hours, both acquisition and fixed annual are 10% of what they would be if you owned the aircraft on your own. 90% of those costs are covered by others.
